Resume

  1. Find five resume templates, choose one or pick a template from Vmock.
  2. Put your information in the template and consider having some of the following sections: Degree, Work Experience, Projects, Awards, Society Involvement, Volunteer Experience, Courses, and Skills. It MUST be down to one page.
  3. Run your resume through VMock and fix any issues. Use VMock to Review Your Resume and reach yellow zone.
  4. Review your resume with two peers and fix what they suggest.

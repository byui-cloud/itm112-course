Week 1 Assignment: Linux Distributions Research

Overview

Task: Create a list of current processes running on your computer.

Purpose: This activity will allow you to recognize the resources required to make an operating system function.

Instructions

  1. Open the task manager (Windows/PC), activity monitor (Mac/Apple), or whichever similar interface the operating system on your computer uses to display current processes and resource utilization. Take a screenshot.
  2. This may require some individual searching and research if you do not know how to do this on your current computer and operating system.
  3. Take screenshots of the following information on your computer:
    • List of current processes running. This is from whatever OS you are running natively.
    • How much RAM is currently being used on your computer.
    • The current free space and the total capacity of your main hard drive.
  4. Submit your screenshots by the due date.

Week 1 Assignment: Linux Distributions Research

Overview 

  • Task: Create a list of current Linux desktop distros and list their strengths and focuses.
  • Purpose: This activity will allow you to better understand how Linux is utilized in various spaces.

Instructions

  1. Conduct a web search for the following terms and topics. You may use conventional search engines or large language models (AI) in your research.
    • Open-source
    • DISTRO
    • Linux Distros
  2. Create a document that includes answers to the following prompts:
    • What does open-source mean in the context of software? What are the relative benefits and difficulties associated with open-source software?
    • What is a DISTRO?
    • Identify five different Linux distros and list their respective strengths and focuses.
  3. Submit your completed document by the due date.

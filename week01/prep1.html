Computer Basics

Task Overview

Complete a short quiz covering the basics of computer construction and function.

Purpose

This activity will allow you to identify and remedy any knowledge gaps you have on the basics of computers.

Instructions

  1. Begin the quiz and complete it to the best of your ability.
  2. Identify any questions you answered incorrectly. Research those topics.
  3. Retake the quiz as necessary. You may attempt this quiz a total of three times.

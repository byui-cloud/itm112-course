Prepare: Most Used Applications

Task Overview

Identify the applications you use most on your computer in your preferred OS.

Purpose

Learn how to find open-source Linux software similar to your most used applications on your current OS.

Instructions

  1. Think about the five applications you use most in your preferred operating system (eg. browser, VS Code, etc.).
  2. Conduct an internet search to find Linux-compatible, open-source alternatives of your top five applications.
    • We will install them in an upcoming assignment.
  3. Create a document that lists your top five applications and the names of Linux-compatible alternatives you could try.
  4. Submit your list of top five applications and Linux alternatives by the due date.

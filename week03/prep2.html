Prepare: Web Servers and NGINX

Task Overview

Use AI tools to learn about and practice with web servers such as NGINX in Linux.

Purpose

Know why Linux is important for web servers and NGINX.

Instructions

  1. Open the AI model/application of your choice (Gemini, ChatGPT, Cluade, Co-Pilot, etc.) to complete the following tasks for each part of the assignment:
    • Copy/paste the provided prompt into the AI chat.
    • Read the response to the prompt.
    • Complete any other steps in each of the parts of the assignment.
  2. Submit your screenshots for each part of the assignment by the due date.
  3. Part 1: Ask AI the following:
    • "What percent of web servers in the world are run on Linux?"
    • Study the results, ask a follow up question, and take a screenshot.
  4. Part 2: Ask AI the following:
    • "What have been the benefits of having Linux be open source?"
    • Study the results, ask a follow up question, and take a screenshot.
  5. Part 3: Ask AI the following:
    • "What is NGINX and why would you need it?"
    • Study the results, ask a follow up question, and take a screenshot.

Submission

Submit your screenshots for each part of the assignment in one document. Include your name and the date.

Make sure to include your follow up questions to learn more and to receive the points for it.

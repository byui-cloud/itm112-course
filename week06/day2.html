Ask your favorite AI to: "Update your AWS server and ssh in with your .pem file."

Next Steps

  • Start your AWS Learned Lab and turn on your instance.
  • Find the .pem file that you downloaded when you created your AWS server (it is probably in your download folder)
  • In your Mac/Apple terminal or in your Windows PowerShell terminal, ssh into your AWS Ubuntu Linux (AWS must be used and you need to ssh in with your .pem).
  • 'cd' into the folder where your .pem file is located.
  • If you are on a Mac/Apple, you might need to use the 'chmod' command to change the permissions on the .pem file.
    • 1. Example: chmod 400 yourkeyfile.pem
  • Use the ssh command to connect to your AWS server.
    • 2. Example: ssh -i "UbuntuDCV-101.pem" ubuntu@ec2-42-252-172-18.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com

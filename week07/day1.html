Week 7: Hands-on Exercise: Processes

Overview

Prerequisite: Open Ubuntu Desktop (AWS or Local VM but you need the GUI - where you can use the mouse).

Task: Launch, Suspend, and Kill processes

Purpose: This activity will allow you to better understand how to suspend and kill processes.

Instructions

  • Read: the Hands-on Exercise Scenarios you generate with your favorite AI using the prompts below.
  • Complete: each part of the exercise in your Linux system (AWS or local VM).
